Helmet Rule in India – Updated Penalties (2025)

Wearing a helmet is not just a safety measure, it’s the law.

✔ Helmet Rules

  • Both rider and pillion must wear helmets.
  • Helmet must have ISI mark.
  • Improper or half helmets may be fined.

✔ Fines (may vary by state)

  • No helmet: ₹500–₹1000
  • Pillion without helmet: ₹500–₹1000
  • Dangerous driving without helmet: higher penalties

✔ Why Helmet is Important

  • 80% of serious head injuries happen in two-wheeler accidents.
  • Helmet can reduce the risk of death by 70%.

✔ Tips

  • Wear helmet strap properly.
  • Avoid plastic toy helmets.
  • Use reflective stickers for night riding.
