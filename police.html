For lost phone, PAN, Aadhaar or other documents:

  • File a lost report (online or at police station).
  • Block SIM through your mobile operator.
  • For smartphones, use tracking / remote erase options if enabled.
  • Use the police acknowledgement when applying for duplicate documents.
