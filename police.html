Police Help & FIR Basics

Understand FIR, complaints, online reports and what your basic rights are.

  • FIR: For serious criminal offences. Investigation is required.
  • Written complaint: General complaint; police may register FIR or NC after review.
  • NC (Non-cognizable): Minor offence; police need court permission to fully investigate.

For serious crime, threat to life, sexual offences etc., insist on an FIR and note the FIR number.
