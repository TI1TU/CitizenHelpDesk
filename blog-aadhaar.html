Aadhaar Address Change – Step-by-Step (2025 Update)

If you recently moved to a new house or city, you should update your Aadhaar address. Here’s a simple guide.

✔ What You Need

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Address proof (e.g., rent agreement, electricity bill, bank statement)
  • Phone number linked with Aadhaar

✔ How to Update Address Online

  1. Visit the Aadhaar Self Service Update Portal.
  2. Log in using your Aadhaar + OTP.
  3. Select Update Address.
  4. Upload your address proof.
  5. Review the preview screen.
  6. Submit.

✔ Processing Time

Usually 3–7 days.

✔ Track Status

You can track using your URN (Update Request Number).
