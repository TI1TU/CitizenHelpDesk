How to Pay Traffic Challan Online – Full Guide (2025)

Paying a traffic challan online is now very easy. You don’t need to visit any police station or RTO office. Here is a simple step-by-step guide.

✔ What You Need

  • Vehicle Number
  • Challan Number (optional)
  • Mobile number
  • UPI / Debit Card / Netbanking

✔ Step-by-Step Process

  1. Open the official challan website (your state traffic police or e-challan portal).
  2. Click “Check Challan Status”.
  3. Enter:
    • Vehicle Number or
    • Challan Number or
    • Driving Licence Number
  4. Your challans will appear on the screen.
  5. Click Pay Now.
  6. Choose UPI/Card/Netbanking.
  7. After payment, download or screenshot your receipt.

✔ Common Reasons for Challans

  • No helmet
  • No seatbelt
  • Wrong parking
  • Triple riding
  • Red light jumping

✔ Tips

  • Always check the domain (must be .gov or .nic).
  • Never pay through WhatsApp links.
  • Keep your digital receipt safe.
