Lost Your PAN Card? Here’s Exactly What to Do

Losing your PAN card can be stressful, but the process to get a reprint is very simple.

✔ Step 1: File a Lost Report

  • If stolen, file an Online FIR for Lost Document.
  • If misplaced, this step is optional.

✔ Step 2: Apply for Reprint

  1. Open the NSDL/UTI portal.
  2. Choose Reprint of PAN.
  3. Enter Aadhaar + PAN.
  4. Pay the small fee.
  5. PAN will be printed and delivered to your address.

✔ Digital PAN Available

Even without printing, you get e-PAN, which is accepted everywhere.
