Common questions

Can police take my bike keys?
In normal checking, they should not forcibly keep your keys or documents without reason.
Is DigiLocker document valid?
Yes, in most places digital RC, DL and other documents from DigiLocker or mParivahan are accepted.
How do I check my traffic challan?
Use your state traffic portal, e-Challan website or mParivahan app with your vehicle number.
