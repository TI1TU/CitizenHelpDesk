Essential Documents & IDs

Short guides for Aadhaar, PAN, voter ID, driving licence, RC and passport.

Use: Identity + address proof for most services.

  • Address change, name correction, mobile update.
  • Update via myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in or Aadhaar centre.
  • Always carry masked Aadhaar or e-Aadhaar, not photocopies everywhere.

üí≥PAN Card

Use: Tax, bank, investments, high-value transactions.

  • Apply / reprint via NSDL or UTIITSL portals.
  • Link with Aadhaar if required by law.
  • Use e-PAN PDF for many online services.

RC: Proof that vehicle is registered in your name.

  • Duplicate RC if original is lost (often needs police report).
