1. Always wear your helmet

A helmet is like a strong protective hat. If the bike falls or something hits you, the helmet protects your brain. Think of it like a superhero shield for your head.

2. The person behind you must also wear a helmet

The person sitting at the back can fall if the bike stops suddenly. A helmet keeps them safe too. Both people need protection.

3. Never take 3 people on a bike

A bike is made only for 2 people. With 3 people, the bike shakes, becomes unbalanced, and can fall easily. It‚Äôs like putting 3 people on a small chair ‚Äî it will fall.

4. Use indicators when you turn

6. Don‚Äôt use loud, modified exhaust

7. Never use mobile phone while riding

Your eyes, hands, and mind must stay on the road. Using a phone is like reading a book while walking ‚Äî you will bump into something.

8. Keep distance from other vehicles

If the vehicle in front stops suddenly, you get time to stop safely. Like walking behind someone ‚Äî if you walk too close, you bump into them.

9. Ride in a straight line, not zig-zag

Zig-zag riding confuses drivers and causes accidents. Ride smooth and straight like drawing a straight line in your notebook.
