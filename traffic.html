Q. Can police seize my licence?
In serious offences they may take DL details or keep the card and issue a receipt. Rules vary by state.

Q. Can I record the interaction?
Yes. You can calmly record audio/video from a safe distance without interfering.

Q. Where to complain?
Use state traffic police helpline, email, or public grievance portal with vehicle & officer details.
