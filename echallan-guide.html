E-Challan in India (2025)

How to check, verify, and pay your online traffic challans safely.

üìÑ What Is an E-Challan?

An e-challan is a digital traffic fine issued by traffic police through:

  • CCTV cameras
  • Speed radar systems
  • Red light detection systems
  • Manual entry by police during checking
  • Wrong parking cameras

You may get an SMS or the challan will appear directly on Parivahan.

üîé How to Check Your E-Challan Online

  1. Go to the Parivahan E-Challan Portal.
  2. Select Check Challan Status.
  3. Enter your vehicle number / DL number / challan number.
  4. View details: date, time, location, photos, fine amount.
  5. Pay online using secure payment gateway.

Important: Never pay through random SMS/WhatsApp links.
